Amazon's 3.5% FBA surcharge went live April 17th.
Here's the 5-step framework we're using for every client.

The reactions I've seen range from "this is devastating" to "this is nothing." The truth depends entirely on your specific business — and whether you ran your numbers before April 17th or after.

Here's the framework VASO Group is using to evaluate impact:

  • 1
    Quantify before you qualify. Pull Fee and Economics Preview and Revenue Calculator data. Know your actual dollar exposure by ASIN before forming an opinion.
  • 2
    Segment by margin band. High-margin ASINs absorb this easily. Thin-margin SKUs need a different strategy — price adjustment, bundling, or FBM.
  • 3
    Model MCF separately. The surcharge extends to Multi-Channel Fulfillment on May 2. If Amazon fulfills your DTC site, that's a separate cost center.
  • 4
    Don't compromise Buy Box strategy. Reactive price increases that protect margin can crater organic ranking and Buy Box win rate. Don't trade short-term margin for long-term position.
  • 5
    Look for the opportunity. Every fee change creates a shake-up. Underprepared sellers make moves that open gaps. Be the brand that fills them.

Amazon's fee environment will keep evolving. The brands that compound their advantage are the ones with real account management infrastructure — not just "someone who checks the dashboard."

If your team is scrambling right now, that's worth examining. Happy to discuss how VASO Group approaches this. DM or visit vasogroup.com.
